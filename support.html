Common Topics

Joining a round
To join a round, tap the Join tab and enter the 6-character code shared by the round creator. Make sure you have an account and are signed in first.

Scoring modes
FairwayLive supports stroke play, net handicap scoring (WHS), and match play. The round creator chooses the scoring mode when the round is created.

Adding a course
If your course isn't in the database, tap Add Course when selecting a course during round setup. Any signed-in user can add or edit course data.

Deleting your account
You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Settings screen inside the app. This action is immediate and cannot be undone.

Forgot your password
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" to receive a reset link by email. The link expires after 1 hour.
